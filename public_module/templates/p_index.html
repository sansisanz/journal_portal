WELCOME TO JOURNAL HUB

The CU Journal Hub is your central repository for scholarly research from Calicut University. Offering a diverse range of academic journals and articles spanning sciences, humanities, and languages, our platform serves as a vital resource for scholars, educators, and students alike. While we do not currently implement a peer review system, each submission undergoes meticulous evaluation by our Editorial Board to ensure authenticity and relevance. With a commitment to promoting academic discourse and advancing knowledge, CU Journal Hub aims to keep you informed of the latest developments and trends within your field of interest. Explore our curated collection of publications to access valuable insights and contribute to the scholarly community.

About Us

Who we are

Here, we proudly showcase a diverse collection of scholarly articles spanning numerous disciplines. As a hub of intellectual inquiry, Calicut University is committed to fostering a culture of research and knowledge dissemination. Our platform offers a gateway to the latest findings and insights from esteemed researchers within and beyond our academic community. Whether you're a student, academic, or enthusiast, delve into our curated selection of journals to explore cutting-edge research, innovative methodologies, and profound discoveries. Join us in our journey of academic exploration and intellectual growth.

MISSION

To foster academic excellence, intellectual curiosity, and interdisciplinary collaboration by providing a platform for the dissemination of high-quality research and scholarly discourse. Our mission is to advance knowledge across diverse fields, promote critical thinking, and empower individuals to make meaningful contributions to society.

VISION

We envision our journal website as a premier hub for academic exploration and innovation, fostering a vibrant community of scholars, researchers, and students. With boundless curiosity and collaboration, we aim to drive positive change and empower future thought leaders, shaping a brighter tomorrow through the transformative power of knowledge.
